How to Play

Use arrow keys or WASD to move your spaceship

Press spacebar to shoot

Press E to activate special weapon

Avoid or destroy enemies to earn points and survive

Chain kills for combo multipliers

Defeat the boss every 5 levels

Collect powerups for special abilities:

  • Extra Life
  • Rapid Fire
  • Shield
  • Double Shot
  • Triple Shot
  • Special Weapon
